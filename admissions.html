Local BD matrix

Bangladesh admission timeline

Engineering, medical, and business-school entrance tests generally follow this rough annual cycle after HSC results are published.

Engineering (BUET / CUET / KUET)

Public
  • HSC results published (July–Aug)
  • Admission circular & online form (Aug–Sep)
  • Admission test (Sep–Oct)
  • Merit list & subject choice (Oct–Nov)

Medical (MBBS)

Public
  • HSC results published (July–Aug)
  • DGHS application window (Sep)
  • MCQ-based admission test (Oct)
  • Merit list & counselling (Nov–Dec)

IBA, University of Dhaka

Public
  • Circular published (Sep–Oct)
  • Written test — English, Math, Analytical (Nov)
  • Viva voce for shortlisted candidates (Dec)
  • Final admission (Jan)

Private universities (NSU / BRACU / IUB)

Private
  • Rolling admission, 4 intakes/year (Jan/May/Sep + Summer)
  • University-specific test or SAT/O-A-Level waiver
  • Scholarship assessed on HSC/O-A-Level GPA
  • Seat confirmation on fee payment
